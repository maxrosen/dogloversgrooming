Dog Lovers Grooming Salon

Dog Lovers Grooming Salon offers a better way to have your pet groomed. We are skilled professionals and do not assembly line groom. Rest assured, your pet is given individual attention and love!

We are one of the only salons in the area offering split-shift grooming. Morning or afternoon visits are available Tuesday thru Saturday by appointment only.

Upon your pet's first visit to the salon, a vaccine history must be presented. A yearly check of the vaccine history will be required. It's for your pet's safety as well as our other visitors. We require all animals entering the salon be vaccinated with the following:

Rabies (1 or 3 year)
Distemper (1 or 3 year)
Bordatella (6 months or 1 year)
Full Service Grooming Includes:

Bath, Blow Dry, and Styling
Ears Plucked (breed specific) and Cleaned
Nails Trimmed and Filed
Breed Specific Hair Cuts, Puppy Cuts or what YOU want.
Special Occasion Bow or Bandana
Optional Services Include:

Flea treatments
Teeth Brushing
Medicated Shampoos and Creme Rinse Conditioners
Gland Expression

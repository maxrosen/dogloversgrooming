Dog Lovers Grooming Salon

Dog Lovers Grooming Salon and its employees sets its sights on maintaining the highest standards in the care and grooming of your pet.

Our constant concern is the humane treatment of any animal in our care!
Stephanie Saster Briggs, Owner - Stephanie Saster Briggs is a lifelong Franklin resident and has been a professional groomer for over 14 years. Mrs. Briggs has been a member of the New England Pet Grooming Professionals (NEPGP) for over 12 years and has been a Registered Member of the National Dog Groomers Association of America (NDGAA) for 6 years. Mrs. Briggs has worked with various area shelters donating her services for animals who need a home. She also donates her services to the Franklin Police Department K9 Unit. When she is not busy at the salon, Mrs. Briggs continues honing her education and skills attending various seminars and trade shows which include Intergroom. Mrs. Briggs love for all animals is evident in every groom she does.
Lisa Saster Lloyd, Groomer - Lisa Saster Lloyd is a Franklin resident and has been a professional groomer for over 7 years. Mrs. Lloyd is a member of the New England Pet Grooming Professionals (NEPGP). Mrs. Lloyd has attended various seminars and trade shows, including Intergroom, to perfect her skills.
